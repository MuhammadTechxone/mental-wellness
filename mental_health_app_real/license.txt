## **8. Key Advantages of This Implementation**

### ✅ **Pipeline-Based Architecture**
- Single `mental_health_pipeline.pkl` file contains both vectorizer and classifier
- No separate vectorizer loading needed
- Ensures transformation consistency

### ✅ **Sentence-Level Analysis**
- Granular detection of mixed states
- User can see exactly what triggered each prediction
- Builds trust through transparency

### ✅ **Production-Ready Features**
- Cached model loading for performance
- Session state management
- Error handling throughout
- Downloadable reports

### ✅ **Clinical Safety**
- Priority flagging of suicidal content
- Clear risk level classification
- Prominent crisis resources
- Multiple disclaimers
